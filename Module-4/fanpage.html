Bio

Gorillaz are a British virtual band created in 1998 by musician Damon Albarn and artist Jamie Hewlett.
The band primarily consists of four animated members: Stuart "2-D" Pot, Murdoc Niccals, Noodle, and
Russel Hobbs. Their fictional universe is explored through music videos, interviews, and other short
cartoons. In reality, Albarn is the only permanent musical contributor, and often collaborates with
other musicians.
